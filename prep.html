Gap & Go — How to play it
Steps
1
Pre-market: find stocks gapping 4%+ on a news catalyst (earnings, upgrade, PR)
2
Mark the pre-market high. This is your key level at the open
3
At 9:30 watch the first 1–2 candles. Let the initial spike happen — do not chase
4
Buy the first pullback candle that holds above VWAP or the pre-market high
5
Stop below the low of the pullback candle. Target: prior resistance or high of day
6
If it reclaims and breaks pre-market high with volume — full send

ORB 15-min — How to play it
Steps
1
Let the first 15 minutes trade. Do not enter during this window — just watch
2
Mark the exact high and low of the 9:30–9:45 candle. This is your range
3
Set alerts at both levels. At 9:44 be ready — you want to react the instant the candle closes
4
If price breaks above the range high on volume: go long. Stop below range high
5
If price breaks below the range low on volume: go short. Stop above range low
6
Best when the breakout aligns with the pre-market trend direction

VWAP Reclaim — How to play it
Steps
1
Find a stock that dipped below VWAP but is showing signs of stabilizing
2
Watch for a candle that closes back above VWAP with increasing volume
3
Enter on the candle that reclaims VWAP or the first pullback to VWAP after reclaim
4
Stop below VWAP — if it loses VWAP again the setup is failed
5
Target: prior high, next resistance level, or HOD
6
Strongest when the broader market (SPY) is also above its VWAP
Indicators

Best time
⏱
9:45–11:30 AM and 1:00–2:30 PM ET. Avoid the choppy midday 11:30–1:00 window
Breakout — How to play it
Steps
1
Identify a stock consolidating in a tight range near a key resistance level (prior high, round number)
2
The tighter and longer the consolidation the better — coiled spring effect
3
Watch for a candle that closes above resistance with a volume spike (2x average or more)
4
Enter on the breakout candle or the first pullback retest of the broken level
5
Stop below the breakout level — if it fails back below, the breakout is false
6
Target: measured move (height of the base added to breakout point)
